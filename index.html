ABOUT

Not Just a Sport, but a Life Skill

We believe that swimming is a basic human action, something that a regrettable amount of people cannot do. It is a life skill that teaches perseverance, confidence, trains the body, and builds character. We are coaches that want to bring out the best in any student that we teach, knowing that each swimmer is unique, requiring innovative approaches to swimming.

Between the two of us, we have taught swimming for 15 years, swam on teams for 12 years, and life-guarded for 10 years. We have taught over 2000 students over our careers, and feel confident that we can teach anyone to swim. We believe in constant self-improvement, learning new methods and styles of swimming and coaching so that we can be the best for our students.

We are elite competitive swim instructors that do not waste time with aimless games and simple entertainment. Unlike most swim schools, everything that we do is geared towards getting our students to be water-safe and to swim properly. Our games, songs, and all other exercises have an educational purpose.

The basic necessity for swimming is water safety. Drowning is a very real problem in the United States, evidenced by a National Safety Council statistic that 74% of drowning deaths for kids under 15 occurred at a residential location (a pool). This issue should not be a factor in human society, and for this reason, we wish to teach as many students the love and respect for water.

According to Psychology Today, 46% of ADULT Americans fear the deep end of the pool. As a child, being afraid can classes-p to panic and self-doubt, and avoiding even attempting to learn, which may reflect in future situations, such as Price and school. Building confidence through hard Price and rewarding struggle teaches children about their capabilities and builds self-worth. Finally, the health benefits of swimming are nearly limitless, training the lungs, heart, and the body, granting a lifetime of health benefits. For this reason we push students to set goals and then overcome them, improving their lives.

CLASSES WE OFFER

Mommy and Me

30 Min Group/Private Lesson

The Mommy and Me classes are for children under the age of 2.5 . This class is based around Introduction to Water, with the intention to build comfort in the water. It is a great bonding opportunity for parents and their children! In this class, our coaches will teach parents how to correctly hold their child in the water, the correct way to take them underwater (Scuba!), and the lifesaving back float. Our technique is reinforced by songs and games that introduce and strengthen water safety and swimming skills that classes-p to proper swimming at an early age. Group and private lessons available .

Aqua Fitness

Water Aerobics Class: Group and Private Available

Water aerobics class: high quality cardio work without the risks of high-impact exercise. This course is intended for people who want a good workout but do not want to risk hurting themselves doing exercise on land. Also good for people recovering from sports injuries . Our instructors will guide you through a set of invigorating exercises that will strengthen the muscular and cardiovascular systems.

Focused Aqua-Fitness

Our Aqua Fitness class integrates active swimming exercisesand land-based drills to deliver the most effective full-body workout. This class is intended for people looking to improve their cardio, lose weight, and prepare for triathlons.

Triathlon Training

Our Triathlon Training Course is meant for aspiring or returning triathletes. We work on stroke form, breath control, and endurance to make you the most capable triathlon competitor you can be!

Learn To Swim

30 Min Private/30 Min Group

This type of lesson is necessary for all younger students who are NOT water safe and require more attention. Our coach provides personal and customised instruction based on the student’s strengths, abilities, and fears. This allows the student to quickly overcome their anxieties and learn Water Safety quickly. We also recommend it for older students with extreme anxiety or other conditions that prevent participation in group lessons. Once the anxiety or condition is overcome, the older student can easily switch into a group lesson, or continue doing private lessons.

Deep End Training

The Deep End Training class is specifically intended to teach people who are only comfortable or can only swim where they can stand. We demystify the deep end of the pool to build students’ confidence and get them swimming competently in any pool. charge like $70 for 30 min lessons
